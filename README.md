# ShelfLight-ESP32
Simple shelf lighting using addressable LEDs
## This is the simplest and clearest guide
The most detailed and simple guide to installing and setting up lighting for your shelf.
## What you need:
  - As much addressable LED strip as you want
  - ESP32 (any version)
  - 3 wires
  - Soldering iron (or a lighter with solder, idk)
  - Power cable for your ESP32
  - Working Internet and Telegram
## What you get
  Super cool lighting for your shelf—you can control its color and state via Telegram.
